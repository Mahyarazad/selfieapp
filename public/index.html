The Selfie App


Hello and Welcome to the Selfie App. This is selfie app therapy! you could write down your feeling and,
come back later checkout your previous status. Rewinding the time and observing yourself could be help!
The app simply captures your portrait photo, stores that image in Amazon S3 storage alongside the geolocation
information in Postgres database accessible here. Also, "face-api.js" a JavaScript API for face detection and,
face recognition in the browser and nodejs with tensorflow.js was added to this app.
